Improve Your Court Forms

Get insights into how to make your PDF court form simpler, more accessible, and more usable by self represented litigants.

Free and Easy

Upload a PDF (with form fields) and let our tool do the rest. There is no cost to you.

Expert Validated

We talked to dozens of court and usability experts and gathered 24,000 court forms from across the country to build our recommendations.

RateMyPDF is a project of Suffolk University Law School's Legal Innovation and Technology Lab.

How the score works: RateMyPDF measures key metrics like readability, length and number of fields, sentence structure, and density of fields on the page to compare your form to forms from around the country and to calculate a score. AI-powered tools recommend easy wins that can help you improve your form for future litigants.
