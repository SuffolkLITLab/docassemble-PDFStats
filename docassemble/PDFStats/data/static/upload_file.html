Suffolk LIT Lab: Rate My PDF

Rate your PDF form

Select a PDF (with form fields) to upload, and RateMyPDF will give you a score and detailed recommendations to make your form simpler and easier for a person to complete. Note: RateMyPDF doesn't work with Adobe LiveCycle forms just yet (but we have some ideas in the works.)

RateMyPDF is part of the Suffolk LIT Lab's broader Document Assembly Line project along with our Form Explorer.
RateMyPDF uses machine learning, expert suggestions, and GPT-3 to give you concrete, actionable information that can make your court form better. RateMyPDF is still in beta, but you can start experimenting with it now.
